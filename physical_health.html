Physical Health Resources

Welcome to Physical Health Resources

Your physical health is a cornerstone of your success as a student. The University of Michigan provides a variety of resources to help you maintain your physical well-being.

University Health Service (UHS)

Health Service (UHS) offers comprehensive medical care for students, including routine check-ups, immunizations, and specialist referrals. Visit UHS to ensure you stay in optimal health during your academic journey.

Nutrition and Wellness

Discover resources for maintaining a balanced diet, including campus dining options and nutrition counseling. Proper nutrition is essential for sustained energy and focus.

Recreational Sports and Fitness

Recreational Sports provides opportunities to stay active through fitness programs, group classes, and sports clubs. Explore gym facilities and outdoor activities to keep your body in top shape.
