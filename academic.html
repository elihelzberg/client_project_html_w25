Academic Resources

Welcome to Academic Resources

Achieving academic success requires the right support and tools. The University of Michigan offers various resources to help you excel in your studies and reach your goals.

Study Spaces

Find quiet and collaborative study spaces across campus, including libraries, lounges, and designated areas. Learn more about Study Spaces.

BSI Advising

Meet with BSI advisors to discuss academic planning, course selection, and career guidance. They are here to support you every step of the way. Learn more about BSI Advising.

Tutoring Services

Utilize tutoring services for individual and group sessions to strengthen your understanding of course material. Learn more about Tutoring Services.
