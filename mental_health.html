Mental Health Resources

Welcome to Mental Health Resources

Maintaining your mental health is critical for success. The University of Michigan provides a variety of services to support your mental well-being and ensure you thrive during your academic journey.

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

CAPS offers individual counseling, group therapy, workshops, and crisis intervention services to help you navigate challenges and maintain mental well-being. Learn more about CAPS.

Wellness Coaching

Work with a wellness coach to develop personalized strategies for managing stress, time, and overall well-being. Learn more about Wellness Coaching.

Mindfulness Resources

Participate in mindfulness and meditation sessions to cultivate a calm and focused mind. Explore resources to integrate mindfulness into your daily life. Learn more about Mindfulness Resources.

Support Groups

Join peer support groups for shared experiences and collective coping strategies. These groups provide a safe space to connect and grow together. Learn more about Support Groups.
